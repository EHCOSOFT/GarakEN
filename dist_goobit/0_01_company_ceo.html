    Message from the CEO

    2. About the Corporation
    3. CEO's Message

    Greetings, I am Moon Young-pyo, CEO of Seoul Agro-Fisheries & Food Corporation.

    I sincerely welcome everyone who visits our website.

    Our corporation operates Garak Market, as well as the agricultural and fishery wholesale markets at Gyeongdong, Yangjae, and more. These markets are crucial in supplying around 2,050 tons of agricultural and fishery products daily, providing citizens of Seoul with fresh and safe food ingredients.

    We are committed to efficiently operating the markets, ensuring fair trade, and enhancing logistics and distribution systems for the benefit of both producers and consumers. By modernizing and digitizing our distribution processes, we aim to become a leader in smart logistics and digital transformation.

    Through continuous innovation, we are developing a distribution network that not only serves as a fresh food hub but also offers diverse cultural experiences, such as themed spaces, exhibitions, and events for everyone to enjoy.

    We deeply appreciate your ongoing support and interest, and we will continue to strive to be a trusted corporation, always focusing on quality service for our customers.

    Moon Young-pyo, CEO of Seoul Agro-Fisheries & Food Corporation
