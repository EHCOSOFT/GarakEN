    Facility Overview
    • Garak Mall is a large-scale complex that provides fresh agricultural, fishery, and food products in a comfortable and modern environment. The mall consists of a sales building, themed halls, and an office building. It is designed to optimize convenience for customers with easy access and efficient logistics services.
