About Garak Mall
Korea’s largest
comprehensive food
market, alive with diverse

Garak Mall is located within Garak Wholesale Market, covering a total area of 211,955㎡.

GARAK MALL is open 365 days a year to serve the citizens of Seoul.
Convenient transportation and parking

The Sales Building and four themed halls where you can experience Korea’s representative food and culinary culture, along with a large market offering fresh and diverse agricultural, fishery, and livestock products with excellent accessibility. It is directly connected to Garak Market Station on Subway Lines 3 and 8 and offers a comfortable environment with parking spaces for 2,050 vehicles.

The largest comprehensive food ingredients market in Korea

A one-stop comprehensive food ingredients center connected to agricultural and fishery products. A market where you can purchase fresh and safe agricultural and fishery products auctioned at Garak Market, as well as various processed foods and kitchen supplies, all in one place.

A hub of Seoul's culinary culture

A themed area filled with diverse food and entertainment options. Features multipurpose spaces like stages, exhibition halls, tasting events, and banquet facilities.

Garak Mall Guide
  • Sales Building
  • Themed Halls (1-5)
  • Office Building (SAFF Building)

With 1,142 stores specializing in fresh produce, seafood, dried goods, meat, and food ingredients, visitors can purchase a wide variety of food products.

  • Fresh Produce (Basement 1)
  • Seafood (1st Floor)
  • Dried Goods (1st Floor)
  • Meat (1st Floor)
  • Food Wholesale Market (2nd Floor)
  • Kitchen Supplies (2nd Floor)
  • Specialty Restaurants (3rd Floor)

The themed halls consist of five buildings that bring together unique retail facilities and dining areas, allowing visitors to experience Korea's representative food markets and culinary culture.

  • Food/Ingredients (Hall 1)
  • Food/Ingredients (Danong Co., Hall 2)
  • Food/Ingredients (Saenong Co., Hall 3)
  • Fish Center (Hall 5, 1st floor)
  • Specialty Restaurants (Halls 5, 2nd-3rd floors)

Convenience stores, banks, childcare facilities, libraries, cooking studios, and medical facilities are available to provide convenience for visitors.

  • Bank (1st floor)
  • Convenience store/retail shop (1st, 5th floors)
  • Pharmacy (1st, 5th floors)
  • Convention Center (Wedding Hall, 2nd floor)
  • Cooking Studio (4th floor)
  • Garak Mall Library (4th floor)
  • Hospital (5th floor)
