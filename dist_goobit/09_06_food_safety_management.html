    Safety Inspection Procedure
    ① Sampling
    ▶ Sampling based on the sampling plan, usually 1-2 hours before auction
    ② Safety Inspection
    (QuECHERS Test)
    • ▶ Pesticide residue screening conducted in the safety inspection lab (QuECHERS Test).
    ③ Suspension of Distribution
    (Non-compliance)
    • ▶ If contamination is suspected : Suspend distribution of the affected agricultural product.
    • ▶ If deemed safe : Allow distribution to proceed.
    ④ Request and Conduct
    Detailed Inspection
    • ▶ Suspected products are sent to the Seoul Health and Environment Research Institute for a detailed inspection.
    • - Tests include analysis of 470 types of pesticides and harmful substances.
    ⑤ Notification of Detailed
    Inspection Results
    • ▶ Results are communicated by the Seoul Health and Environment Research Institute between 2 PM and 4 PM on the same day.
    ⑥ Follow-up Actions
    • ▶ If contamination is confirmed : Disposal, sales restrictions for a specified period, notification to relevant authorities.
    • ▶ If safe : Lift distribution suspension and implement compensation measures if necessary

    ※ QuECHERS Method (Quick, Easy, Cheap, Effective, Rugged, Safe): A rapid multi-residue analysis method for detecting harmful substances in agricultural products, as specified in the "Analytical Methods for Harmful Substances in Agricultural Products" under food safety regulations.
